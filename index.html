Why Ambdere?

“ambdere” is a fictional word that a friend of mine created years ago while joking.

I liked the sound of this word and I started to use it as my username in many websites and online games, for I’ve never been skilled in the creation of usernames and nicknames.

Through the years it became a distinctive characteristic of my persona and a sort of personal signature.

So I decided, at last, to buy the domain ambdere.com and to create my personal website on it as a memento of my past and a reminder that we have not the right to mock even the most insignificant thing, for it might has a story to tell.
